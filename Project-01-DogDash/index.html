Our Story

Our mission at DogDASH is to bring assistance and convenience in your family's lives. Our reason for creating this company is to help those in need that are not able to walk their 4 legged friends. Either for help because you're not able to or there is just no time, We got your back!
When you join us, your local trusted walkers will take your dog for a trip with other pets around the neighborhood.

Requirements

  • Owner must supply a reliable leash and collar for their pet.
  • Pet must have no history of attacks or violence towards animals or people.
  • Owner must provide vaccine history for their pet.

Join Us!
